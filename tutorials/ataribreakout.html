How To Play Atari Breakout (JS And HTML)

Yes. You came here from the gamepage so that you can learn to play this game.

First of all, you can only play this game on a computer or laptop. So, it's a big "F" for mobile users.

How To Play:

1. To move the paddle (the blue thingy that the bottom), you can use the the left and right arrow key. You also can use your mouse. Move it left or/and right and it will detect your movement

2. The ball will bounce everywhere (dUh). It will hit a block. Each block the ball hits, you get a point. If the ball doesn't hit the paddle when it bounces back from the block, you lose a life and the next round will start immediately.

3. You have a slight advantage to win after losing a life

4. When you touch the ground, the next round starts. The paddle and the ball will start from the middle and starts bouncing to the right.

The ball will slip through the empty hole of the bricks...

5. When you win it will show a message box like this: "YOU WIN! Play Again? (press OK to play again)"

6. Press "OK" to play again

*Step 5 & 6 also applies if "Game Over"
